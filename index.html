Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Introduction

Forex Calculator Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Saved calculation profiles (account size, risk percentage, currency pairs)
  • Calculation history
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Usage data and interaction with ads

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy

Google Play In-App Billing

Purchase transactions for premium features are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information. Google Play's privacy policy applies to all purchases.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Financial information or trading account details
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or photos

How We Use Information

  • Local data (profiles, history) is used solely to provide app functionality
  • AdMob data is used to serve personalized advertisements
  • Purchase data is used to verify Premium subscription status

Data Security

All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security measures. We implement industry-standard security practices in our app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear calculation history within the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
